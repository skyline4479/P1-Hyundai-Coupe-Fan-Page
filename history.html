History and Evolution of Hyundai Coupe - 4 generations

The Hyundai Coupe is a sports coupe produced by Hyundai from 1996 to 2009, commonly known as the Hyundai Tiburon. It was an affordable sports design car with advanced features that were not available even in more expensive models at that time. The engines were not very powerful, but operated dynamically in urban conditions and were also very fuel-efficient.

The Korean manufacturing is tried and tested, with a high-quality finish and brilliant attention to detail.
